The <span class="fi fi-c-commonwealth fis"></span> **Federal Inspectorate** is a constituent body of the [[Ministry of the Commonwealth]] responsible for conducting independent inspections, audits and compliance reviews across all federal and republic government operations in [[Vekllei]]. The Inspectorate serves as the Commonwealth's internal oversight mechanism, ensuring ministries and public offices maintain standards while identifying inefficiencies and recommending improvements.

The Inspectorate operates with substantial autonomy from the ministries it inspects, reporting directly to the Commonwealth Directory and Parliament. Inspectors possess broad authority to access facilities, review records and interview staff across all government operations. Unlike criminal investigations conducted by the [[Parliament of Law]], the Inspectorate focuses on administrative compliance, operational efficiency and standards adherence.

Inspection teams are organised by ministry sector, with specialised inspectors trained in the technical requirements of each domain. A typical inspection might examine workplace safety standards, procurement practices, resource allocation efficiency or service delivery quality. The Inspectorate publishes quarterly reports identifying systemic issues and tracking ministry responses to recommendations.

## Constituents

* <span class="fi fi-c-commonwealth fis"></span> **Commonwealth Standards Directorate**: Development and maintenance of administrative standards and best practices for government operations.
* <span class="fi fi-c-commonwealth fis"></span> **Technical Inspection Directorate**: Specialised inspectors for infrastructure, facilities and equipment maintenance standards.
* <span class="fi fi-c-commonwealth fis"></span> **Federal Standards & Inspection College**: Professional development and certification for government inspectors across all specialisations.
* <span class="fi fi-c-commonwealth fis"></span> **Republic Liaison**: Coordination between federal inspectors and republic-level inspection services.
* <span class="fi fi-c-commonwealth fis"></span> **Inspectorate Intelligence Directorate**: Analysis of inspection data to identify systemic problems and emerging issues.

## Departments

* **Department of Ministry Inspections**: Conducts regular and surprise inspections of federal ministry operations across all eight Regional Commonwealths.
  * Commerce Inspection Division
  * Culture Inspection Division
  * Defence Inspection Division
  * Industry Inspection Division
  * Labour Inspection Division
  * Landscape Inspection Division
  * Light and Water Inspection Division
  * Commons Inspection Division
* **Department of Republic Inspections**: Coordinates inspection activities within republic governments and municipal administrations.
  * Republic Government Standards Office
  * Municipal Administration Review Office
  * Local Service Quality Office
  * Republic Compliance Monitoring Office
* **Department of Standards and Compliance**: Develops inspection protocols, maintains compliance standards and tracks ministry responses to recommendations.
  * Office of Standards Development
  * Office of Compliance
  * Office of Programmes & Implementation
* **Department of Special Investigations**: Handles complex inspections requiring extended investigation or multi-ministry coordination.
  * Office of System Failure
  * Office of Consort Review
  * Office of Emergency Inspections
  * Office of Standards Surveillance
