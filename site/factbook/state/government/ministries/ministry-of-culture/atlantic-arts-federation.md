The <span class="fi fi-nas fis"></span> **Atlantic Arts Federation** is a cultural federation and educational institution associated with the [[Ministry of Culture]] responsible for providing tertiary arts education, maintaining performance venues and promoting excellence in the fine arts throughout [[Vekllele]]. The Federation serves as both an educational institution and a cultural centre, and facilitates a variety of programs to benefit the arts. It is partially funded by the Ministry but otherwise autonomous from it, and the Ministry retains no editorial or administrative control.

Members of the federation include schools specialising in different artistic disciplines and professional arts and performance organisations. Through its constituents it arranges exchanges, education programmes, performances, exhibitions and festivals.

## Constituents

* <span class="fi fi-nas fis"></span> **Commonwealth Conservatory of Music:** Advanced musical education and performance training.
* <span class="fi fi-nas fis"></span> **Mayaguana Federal Conservatory:** Major [[Kalina]] school of music at [[CUWI]].
* <span class="fi fi-nas fis"></span> **Atlantic Theatre** Professional training for actors, directors and theatre technicians.
* <span class="fi fi-nas fis"></span> **Atlantic School of Dance:** Classical and contemporary dance education and company associated with the [[University of Karu]] in [[Karu]].
* <span class="fi fi-nas fis"></span> **National Ballet School:** Professional ballet education and company of the [[University of Karu]] in [[Karu]].
* <span class="fi fi-nas fis"></span> **Caribbean Opera Company:** Professional opera performances and vocal training.
* <span class="fi fi-nas fis"></span> **Federation Concert Hall:** Primary performance venue for musical events.
* <span class="fi fi-nas fis"></span> **Commonwealth Artist Exchange:** Exchange, placement and residence programmes for Vekllei artists.
* **[[Commonwealth Art Commission]]:** Government visual arts promotion programmes, including publication of the [[National Design Atlas]].

## Departments

* **Council of Musical Arts**: Oversees the development and promotion of musical education, composition and performance across Vekllei.
  * Congress of Classical Performance
  * Congress of Composition & Theory
  * Congress of Contemporary Music
* **Council of Dramatic Arts**: Manages the training and professional practice of acting, directing and theatrical production.
  * Congress of Acting & Performance
  * Congress of Direction & Production
* **Council of Visual Arts**: Supports and promotes both traditional and contemporary visual arts and artists.
  * Congress of Traditional Arts
  * Congress of Contemporary Practice
* **Council of Atlantic Dance**: Governs the standards and promotion of classical ballet and contemporary dance.
  * Congress of Classical Ballet
  * Congress of Contemporary Dance
