The <span class="fi fi-c-commons fis"></span> **Bureau of Space** maintains governance and infrastructure on the [[Vekllei]] moon, other celestial claims and in outer space. It is a unique government organisation with the fascinating role of facilitating civilian life in Vekllei's offworld settlements. On smaller, scientific settlements like Station Mars and Station Venus, the bureau assumes a direct governance role. On older and more established settlements, it retains a functional administrative approach that supports local government. In the Vekllei Lunar Settlement and Space Station Veletia, the bureau conducts maintenance and improvements, and also manages affairs between councils in the settlements.

Although formal celestial and space diplomacy is conducted through the [[Ministry of the Interior]], the Bureau of Space may conduct regular trade and diplomatic actions on behalf of off-world settlements as a representative of Vekllei citizens in space. The bureau also generally manages or supports any day-to-day affairs of civilian space settlement, including interstellar transport routes and construction material delivery.

## Constituents

* <span class="fi fi-c-commons fis"></span> **Lunar Municipal Council**: Local government of the Vekllei Lunar Settlement.
* <span class="fi fi-c-commons fis"></span> **Venus Public Council**: Administrative board for Station Venus.
* <span class="fi fi-c-commons fis"></span> **Mars Public Council**: Administrative board for Station Mars.
* <span class="fi fi-c-commons fis"></span> **Vekllei Celestial Development Corporation**: Planning and design firm specialising in off-world construction.
* <span class="fi fi-c-commons fis"></span> **Commonwealth Lunar Trade Board**: Trade promotion board for off-world corporations and investment.

## Departments
* **Department of Space Settlements**: Administers governance and provides support for Vekllei's individual off-world colonies.
    * Lunar Commission
    * Mars Commission
    * Venus Commission
    * Stations Commission
* **Department of Policy & Security**: Develops strategic policy for space exploration trade law and scientific missions.
    * Missions & Charts Office
    * Science Office
    * Multilateral Policy Office
    * Trade & Economics Office
    * Law & Protocols Office
* **Department of Operations**: Manages the logistical and infrastructural support for all civilian activities in space.
    * Office of Infrastructure & Habitation
    * Office of Transport & Supply
    * Office of Colonial Administration
