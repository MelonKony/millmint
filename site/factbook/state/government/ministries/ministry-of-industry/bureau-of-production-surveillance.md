The <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Bureau of Production Surveillance** is a bureau of the [[Ministry of Industry]] that tracks what the Commonwealth produces, how efficiently factories operate and whether output meets quality standards. In a [[commons|moneyless economy]] without price signals to indicate productive efficiency, the bureau has to develop alternative metrics for assessing industrial performance. This surveillance extends across everything from automated lots producing electronics to small workshop cooperatives crafting furniture.

The bureau's work differs fundamentally from capitalist quality control because it operates without profit incentives. Rather than maximizing output per worker or minimizing costs, surveillance focuses on whether production serves community needs, operates sustainably and maintains acceptable quality standards. A factory running at 60% capacity might be performing perfectly if demand has been satisfied, while another at 90% capacity could be wasteful if producing unwanted goods. The bureau must distinguish between genuine inefficiency and the natural variation of an economy organised around use rather than exchange.

This monitoring extends beyond individual facilities to track industrial patterns across republics and sectors. The bureau identifies bottlenecks where production lags behind demand, oversupply situations where factories produce more than communities need, and quality issues requiring intervention. Regional disparities receive particular attention, so smaller island republics receive adequate manufactured goods despite limited local production capacity. The data feeds back into planning processes, helping the [[Bureau of Industrial Coordination]] adjust production priorities.

## Constituents

* <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Industrial Statistics Service**: Collection and analysis of production data across all manufacturing sectors. Operates statistical offices in each republic coordinating with local factory councils.
* <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Quality Standards Authority**: Development and enforcement of product quality standards. Conducts inspections and coordinates with [[Bureau of Standards]] on technical specifications.
* <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Production Efficiency Commission**: Analysis of manufacturing efficiency and identification of productivity improvements. Provides technical assistance to underperforming facilities.
* <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Industrial Survey Office**: Regular assessment of production capacity, equipment condition and future manufacturing requirements across all republics.

## Departments

* **Department of Statistics & Analysis**: Collects analyses and reports on production data and industrial trends across the Commonwealth.
  * Office of Data Collection
  * Office of Industrial Statistics
  * Office of Sectoral Analysis
  * Office of Comparative Studies
  * Office of Reporting & Publication
* **Department of Quality & Standards**: Establishes and enforces product quality standards through testing inspection and standards development.
  * Office of Quality Control
  * Office of Product Testing
  * Office of Standards Development
  * Office of Inspection Services
* **Department of Efficiency & Improvement**: Analyses manufacturing efficiency and provides technical assistance to improve industrial productivity.
  * Office of Productivity Analysis
  * Office of Technical Assistance
  * Office of Best Practices
  * Office of Automation Assessment
* **Department of Capacity & Planning**: Assesses current industrial capacity and plans for future manufacturing requirements and equipment needs.
  * Office of Industrial Surveys
  * Office of Equipment Assessment
  * Office of Capacity Planning
  * Office of Future Requirements
