The <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Bureau of Hydrocarbons** is a bureau of the [[Ministry of Industry]] responsible for hydrocarbon resource management across Vekllei's extensive maritime territories. Despite broad adoption of nuclear energy for commercial, industrial and some transportation purposes, petroleum and natural gas remain essential for non-nuclearised transport, plastics manufacturing and chemical feedstocks. The bureau coordinates extraction from offshore platforms, oversees refining facilities and manages strategic reserves distributed across island republics.

The geographic dispersal of Vekllei's territories creates unique challenges for hydrocarbon infrastructure. Major refineries operate in [[Curacao]], [[Kairi]] and [[Hetland]], processing crude oil from Commonwealth offshore fields and occasional imports. The bureau must balance local refining capacity against transportation costs, maintaining fuel depots on smaller islands while avoiding redundant infrastructure. Regional fuel security becomes particularly critical during hurricane season in the Caribbean or winter storms affecting Arctic territories.

Beyond extraction and distribution, the bureau manages the Commonwealth's transition away from hydrocarbon dependence. This includes oversight of synthetic fuel research, coordination with [[NSRE]] on nuclear alternatives and gradual reduction of petroleum use where practical substitutes exist. The work requires balancing immediate energy needs against long-term environmental commitments, a tension that plays out differently across republics with varying industrial profiles and natural resources.

## Constituents

* **[[Commonwealth Oil]]:** National oil and gas company, responsible for all extraction and processing in Vekllei.
* <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Commonwealth Petroleum Commission**: Management of crude oil extraction, processing and strategic reserves. Operates major refineries and coordinates with Maritime Service for offshore platform security.
* <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Commonwealth Natural Gas Commission**: Oversight of natural gas extraction, liquefaction and distribution networks. Manages specialised tanker vessels for inter-island gas transport.
* <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Fuel Distribution Service**: Operation of fuel depot networks, pipeline infrastructure and retail distribution across all republics. Coordinates emergency fuel supplies during disasters.
* <span class="fi fi-c-industry fis"></span> **Synthetic Fuel Research Office**: Development of alternative hydrocarbon sources including coal liquefaction, biomass conversion and synthetic fuel production.

## Departments

* **Department of Extraction & Processing**: Manages the extraction of crude oil and natural gas and oversees its refinement into usable products.
  * Office of Offshore Extraction
  * Office of Onshore Extraction
  * Office of Refining Operations
  * Office of Quality Control
* **Department of Distribution & Reserves**: Oversees the national distribution network strategic stockpiles and emergency fuel supply logistics.
  * Office of Pipeline Management
  * Office of Depot Operations
  * Office of Strategic Stockpiles
  * Office of Emergency Distribution
* **Department of Minor Hydrocarbons**: Conducts research and development into alternative and synthetic hydrocarbon fuel sources.
  * Office of Synthetic Fuel Research
  * Office of Biomass Conversion
  * Office of Coal Liquefaction
  * Office of Technology Transfer
