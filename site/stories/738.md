The CMR IV is a self-fuelled, helium-cooled, fast-spectrum, gas-turbine reactor commonly used in aircraft. 

The core is cast ceramic to reduce vulnerability to vibration, and is moderated by rotating drums. It lasts about 8 years and is simply replaced at the end of its life.
